<p><p>The piezoelectric transformer market is segmented into step-up and step-down transformers, each serving distinct applications. Step-up transformers increase voltage output, making them ideal for devices requiring high energy levels, such as in lighting and power supply applications. Conversely, step-down transformers reduce voltage levels, suitable for consumer electronics and low-power devices requiring efficient energy conversion. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials for compactness and efficiency, contributing to advancements in energy management and power transmission across various industries.</p></p>
