<p><p>The perfume and fragrance market comprises several types, each defined by their concentration of aromatic compounds. Eau de Parfum has a higher concentration (15-20%) and offers a long-lasting scent. Eau de Toilette contains 5-15% concentration, providing a lighter fragrance suitable for daily wear. Eau de Cologne, with about 2-5% concentration, is refreshing and often used as a splash. Eau Fraiche has the lowest concentration (1-3%), delivering a very subtle scent ideal for a quick refresh. Each type caters to varying preferences and occasions.</p></p>
