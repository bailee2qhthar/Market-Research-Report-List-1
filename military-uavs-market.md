<p><p>The military UAV market is categorized into three primary types: fixed-wing, rotary-wing, and hybrid UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs offer long-range capabilities and efficient fuel consumption, making them ideal for surveillance and reconnaissance missions. Rotary-wing UAVs, like drones, provide greater maneuverability and vertical takeoff, suited for tactical operations in confined areas. Hybrid UAVs combine features of both types, delivering versatility in various mission profiles, including transport and strike operations, while benefiting from enhanced endurance and operational flexibility.</p></p>
