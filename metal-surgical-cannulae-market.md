<p><p>The metal surgical cannulae market comprises primarily two types: curved and straight cannulae. Curved cannulae are designed for procedures requiring access to difficult angles, providing enhanced maneuverability and precision in soft tissues. In contrast, straight cannulae are utilized for direct access and straightforward procedures, facilitating efficient fluid drainage or injection. Both types are essential in various surgical applications, including minimally invasive surgeries, ensuring versatility and adaptability in surgical practices to meet diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
