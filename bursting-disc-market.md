<p><p>The bursting disc market encompasses various types, including positive arch, anti-arch, and flat type bursting discs. Positive arch bursting discs are designed to provide precise pressure relief by bending outward, ensuring reliable operation. Anti-arch bursting discs feature an inward curvature that enhances safety and reduces blow-out risks. Flat type bursting discs, with their sleek design, offer high burst pressure and a compact profile, making them suitable for a range of applications. Each type serves specific operational needs in pressure management systems.</p></p>
