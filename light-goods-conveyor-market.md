<p><strong>The Light Goods Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Chain</li><li>Roller (driven or gravity)</li><li>Plastic Type</li><li>Slat Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light goods conveyor market includes various types designed for efficient material handling. Drag chain conveyors utilize a chain to move items along a track, ideal for bulk materials. Roller conveyors can be driven for powered movement or gravity-fed for lightweight goods. Plastic type conveyors feature modular plastic components, offering flexibility and customization. Slat type conveyors use interlocking slats to transport items steadily, suitable for larger or irregularly shaped products, ensuring smooth transitions in production and packaging lines.</p></p>
