<p><p>The automotive engine management sensors market comprises various types that enhance engine efficiency and performance. Mass airflow sensors measure air intake for optimal fuel mixtures. Knock sensors detect engine knock to prevent damage. Crankshaft sensors monitor position and speed for accurate timing. Camshaft sensors ensure synchronization for valve operation. Pressure sensors gauge various parameters like fuel and oil pressure. Temperature sensors monitor engine heat to prevent overheating. Together, these sensors enable precise engine control, improving fuel economy and reducing emissions.</p></p>
